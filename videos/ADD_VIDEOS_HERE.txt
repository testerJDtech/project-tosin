Drop your .mp4 video files in this folder.

Then in index.html, find the `mediaItems` array (near the bottom of the file)
and uncomment / add lines like:

    { type: "video", src: "videos/birthday-toast.mp4" },

Tips:
- .mp4 format works in every browser
- Keep videos under ~50MB each so they load fast
- Videos auto-mute (browsers require this for autoplay)
- Mix freely with photos — the carousel shuffles them randomly
